Welcome to the Global Forum Documentation!
=======================================================

.. image:: /images/logo_main.png

Hello forum members,

At the `Global forum Community <https://forum.periurban.org>`_ , we believe there's nothing more unstoppable than when people come together.

The Groups mission is to create a Global community of diverse individual who will support, challenge, and inspire one another by providing a platform for networking, mentorship and Career Development.

We encourage you to share your knowledge, ask questions, participate in discussions and become an integral part of this little community. Together we become better community and provide our members with a much better experience.

Your group managers are ``Joe Ravetz``. If you have feedback, questions, or would like to get more involved, email ``admin@forum.periurban.org`` .

To email the global group directly use ``members@forum.periurban.org`` . Members of this group can comment publicly & privately on posts, public comments may be emailed to the entire group at the discretion of the original sender.
